INSTRUCTION FOR USE

1. Borrowed Document Button

Click Borrowed Document to view the document you are borrowing.

2. Returned Document

Click Returned Documents to review the books you've already return for.

3. Renew Document Button

Click on Renew Document to renew the document (if any). The period will be extended by 7 days.

4. Search Document Button

Click Search Document to find the document in the library.

5. Discussion Room Button

Click Discussion Room to register to borrow a group discussion room. A group must have a minimum of 5 members.

6. My Profile Button

Click on My Profile to view your personal information.

7. FeedBack Button

Click FeedBack to submit comments to the library. All your feedback is sent anonymously so don't worry.
